# Scrap Tire Disposal — East Carroll Parish, LA

A practical, ready-to-call plan for legally getting rid of **~30–40 mixed scrap tires**
(passenger, farm-truck, and tractor/ag) from East Carroll Parish, Louisiana
(Lake Providence), within roughly an 80-mile radius — plus a few larger hubs a bit
farther out.

- **Full plan:** [PLAN.md](PLAN.md)
- **Printable / shareable version:** [tire-disposal-plan.html](tire-disposal-plan.html)

## The 30-second version

1. **Don't take whole tires to a landfill** — Louisiana bans them from landfills.
   They have to go to a tire dealer, a permitted waste-tire processor, or a parish
   collection point.
2. **The ~30 passenger/farm-truck tires are easy** — a tire shop or Walmart Auto Care
   takes them for roughly **$2–4 each**.
3. **The tractor/ag tires are the expensive part** — budget **$15–60+ each**, or give
   them away free to a farmer (silage covers, feed bunks) and pay nothing.
4. **Best first call:** the East Carroll Parish Police Jury — ask about a scrap-tire
   collection day or designated drop-off (those are often **free**).

> ⚠️ **About the phone numbers:** the automated pass that verifies current addresses
> and phone numbers was stopped early to conserve credits. Treat every specific number
