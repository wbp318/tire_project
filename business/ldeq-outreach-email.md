Mr. Donlon,

My name is William and I farm in East Carroll Parish, in the northeast corner of the state. I'm researching the possibility of starting an LDEQ-permitted waste tire processing operation up here, and I'd value your guidance before I invest any further.

Before I go any further, I want to make sure I'm building toward something the program actually needs and can support.

Three questions I'm hoping you can help with:

3. **Getting started** — Could you send me the current Waste Tire Processor application package and guidance, and let me know the best way to walk through the requirements (financial assurance, site standards, reporting) with your office?

I'm trying to do this the right way from the start. Any time you can spare to point me in the right direction would be much appreciated. I'm happy to talk by phone at your convenience.
